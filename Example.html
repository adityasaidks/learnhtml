Chunky Chocolate Chip Cookie
Dough.Co

"The best Chunky Chocolate Cookie Dough in the world!"

All natural ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups ground almonds
  • 1/2 tsp vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 30 chunk dark chocolate

Instruction

  1. Preheat the oven to 300F
  2. Place dough on tray
  3. Cook 15 minutes or until golden brown
  4. Let cool for 5 minutes
  5. enjoy!

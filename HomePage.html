What are the 5 levels of the he Human Body?

  • The major levels of organization in the body, from the simplest to the most complex are:
  • Atoms
  • Molecules
  • Organelles
  • Cells
  • Tissues
  • Organs
  • Organ Systems
  • Human Organism

About the Human Body?

  • Human body, the physical substance of the human organism, composed of living cells and extracellular materials and organized into tissues, organs, and systems.
